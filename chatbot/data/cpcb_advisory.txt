CPCB Guidelines (India):
- AQI up to 50: Good, minimal impact.
- AQI 51–100: Satisfactory, minor breathing discomfort to sensitive people.
- AQI 101–200: Moderate to Poor, breathing discomfort to people with lungs/heart disease, children, elderly.
- AQI 201–300: Very Poor, may cause respiratory illness.
- AQI 301–400: Severe, serious health impacts.
- AQI 401–500: Hazardous, may trigger health emergencies.

Recommendations:
- Limit outdoor exposure if AQI >150.
- Avoid early morning exercise during high pollution.
- Use masks and purifiers.
- Sensitive groups should remain indoors during severe levels.
