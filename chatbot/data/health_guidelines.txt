General Preventive Health Guidelines for Air Pollution:

1. Limit outdoor activities when AQI > 200.
2. Keep windows and doors closed during peak pollution hours.
3. Use air purifiers with HEPA filters indoors.
4. Drink plenty of water to help flush toxins.
5. Wash face and hands after returning indoors.
6. Avoid burning candles or incense during high AQI days.
7. Use certified N95/PM2.5 masks when going outdoors.
8. For elderly and asthma patients: keep inhalers and medications accessible.
9. Schedule outdoor tasks during late afternoon or after rainfall.
